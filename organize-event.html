PUSH BACK THE DARKNESS
Thank you very much for your interest in organizing an event!
In order to facilitate a broader discussion in cities across Canada, this page describes a template for when and how events can be held which support this initiative.
Event Agenda
The event should start at ~7pm local time on January 29th. Though the duration is up to you as the event organizer, a suggestion on an agenda is as follows:
  • 5 minutes:
    Introductory speech
  • 5 minutes:
    Push Back The Darkness initiative video screening
  • 20 minutes:
    Signing names and messages on the logo poster (see below for details)
  • Between 30 and 60 minutes (variable depending upon the depth and quality of the discussion):
    Discussion of how to make a difference locally
  • 50 minutes:
    Documentary screening, "Your Last Walk In The Mosque"
  • A moment of silence
  • 5 minutes:
    Closing remarks
What we need to know from you
  • That you are interested in hosting an event in your city - contact us at push.back.the.darkness.initiative@gmail.com or at (647) 213-6563
  • Your mailing address
How we will help
  • A 24-inch by 36-inch printout of the Push Back The Darkness logo will be sent to you:
    This poster will be customized with your city and event name, and should be signed by all attendees of your event (along with messages if they choose). Once completed, please take GOOD QUALITY PICTURES and send them to us at the email address above - these will be published nationally to represent your event to the rest of the country!
  • Advertising poster:
    A customized advertising poster for your event will be created for you, in electronic formats suitable for both sharing online and printing, which will include the address and time of your event
  • Event day videos:
    Links to both suggested videos which can be played as part of this event will be sent to you
  • Discussion guide:
    A few suggestions for questions and thoughts to guide the 'how to make a difference locally' discussion will be sent to you
  • Event listing:
    Your event will be listed alongside the others which are occurring across the country on this website
  • Anything else:
    If there is anything else which we can do to assist you with your event, please do not hesitate to contact us - we want to help to make this as easy as possible for you
Our recommendations for hosting this event
  • If possible, host this at a central civic location such as a city hall or library. Hosting the event at a place of worship such as a church or a mosque may make it more difficult for people outside of that community to attend the event
  • Please choose a place of prominence, such as a public atrium in a central civic location, to hang this poster in once completed with the signatures and messages of event attendees
